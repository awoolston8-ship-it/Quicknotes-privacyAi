TL;DR

We collect nothing. Your API key and summaries are stored only in your browser and never leave your device (except transcript text sent to Anthropic's API to generate your summary).

Overview

YouTube QuickNotes AI ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that generates AI-powered summaries of YouTube videos. This Privacy Policy explains what data the Extension accesses, how it is used, and what is never collected or transmitted.

Data We Do Not Collect

YouTube QuickNotes AI does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically, we never collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your YouTube watch history or browsing activity
  • Video transcript content or summary results
  • Your Anthropic API key (it never leaves your device)
  • Crash reports, analytics, or usage statistics
  • Any information about your device or browser

There are no servers, no databases, and no third-party analytics tools associated with this Extension.

Data Stored Locally On Your Device

The Extension stores the following data in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local). This data never leaves your device:

Anthropic API Key

Your API key is saved locally so you don't need to re-enter it each session. It is stored only in your browser and sent directly from your browser to Anthropic's API (api.anthropic.com) when you generate a summary. It is never sent to any other server.

Summary History

When you generate a summary, the results (bullet points, key takeaways, timestamps, and transcript text) are saved locally so you can access them again without re-generating. This data is stored only on your device and never uploaded anywhere.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by:

  • Clicking the 🕐 History icon in the extension panel and deleting individual entries
  • Clicking "Clear" in the extension popup to remove your saved API key
  • Removing the extension from Chrome, which deletes all associated storage

Third-Party Services

Anthropic API

When you click "Generate Summary," the Extension sends the video transcript text to Anthropic's API (api.anthropic.com) to generate a summary. This request is made directly from your browser using your own API key. Anthropic's use of this data is governed by their own Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

YouTube

The Extension reads transcript text from the YouTube page you are currently viewing. It does not interact with the YouTube API, does not access your YouTube account, and does not read any data beyond the transcript of the video you are actively watching.

Chrome Permissions Explained

Permission Why it's needed
activeTab / youtube.com Required to read the transcript text from the YouTube page you are watching
storage Required to save your API key and summary history locally on your device
api.anthropic.com Required to send the transcript to Anthropic's API and receive the generated summary

Children's Privacy

This Extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no data is collected by the Extension at all, there is no risk of inadvertently collecting children's information.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be published here with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Extension after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the Extension's data practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support tab on the extension listing page.
